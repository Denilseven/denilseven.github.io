About me

My name is Denilson and I'm a 16 years old boy who enjoys creating stuff and playing some games.


I remember playing with building blocks and cardboard as a kid, creating whatever my imagination would bring to me, I would build castles, spaceships and even craft toys!


I lived most of my childhood in the countryside and in my first interactions with technology I was fascinated by just how magical it looked. I was used to watching cartoons on a tube TV, but being able to talk to someone from another city in real time and even play "snake" was next level for me at the time.


I'm glad I have the chance to explore this weird, yet fantastic, tech world and study the ins and outs of how things work, even being able to create my own video games!


My favorite things to do are walking in the woods (to get that good ol' vitamin D), learning new things and creating stuff.
